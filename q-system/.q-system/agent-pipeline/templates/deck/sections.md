## Guidance

- **Headlines carry the argument.** A reader should understand the story from headlines alone.
- **One insight per slide.** If you need two points, use two slides.
- **Slide count by type:**
  - Full pitch deck: 8-12 slides
  - Topic deck: 3-5 slides
  - Meeting-specific: 5-8 slides
- **Stats are visual anchors.** Large, bold, isolated. Not buried in body text.
