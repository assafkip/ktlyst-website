# Brand Voice by Channel

> Channel-specific adaptations of the founder's voice.

## LinkedIn
- **Register:** Published (proper caps, punctuation, but still direct)
- **Length:** 150-250 words for posts
- **Structure:** Hook (1-2 sentences) -> Body (3-5 short paragraphs) -> Close (question or sharp statement)
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 at end
- **Never:** Generic CTAs like "Thoughts?" or "Agree?"

## X/Twitter
- **Register:** Punchy, headline-style
- **Length:** Under 280 chars for single tweets
- **Threads:** Each tweet stands alone, 3-5 tweets max
- **Never:** Thread-bait hooks with no payoff

## Medium / Long-form
- **Register:** Published but conversational
- **Length:** 800-1500 words
- **Structure:** 4-6 sections with headers every 200-300 words
- **Open with:** A scar or scene-setting from real experience
- **Close with:** A reflective question, not a pitch

## Email / DMs
- **Register:** Conversational (lowercase ok, short bursts)
- **First line:** Reference something specific about the recipient
- **Never:** "Hope you're well" or "Hope this finds you"
- **Sign-off:** First name only (no Best/Cheers/Regards)
